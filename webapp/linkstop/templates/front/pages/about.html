About Loci Desktops

Loci Desktop is a new way to save & share your favorite websites.

Bookmarks Are Broken

Bookmarks have been around since the birth of the browser, but many people find that even though they have bookmarked a link, they will google for the site rather than find it in the bookmarks.

Desktops Are Better

Do you find that when you enter your pin number in to the ATM, that the pattern the digits makes on the keypad comes to mind, before the digits themselves?

This is because most people recall things better when they are arranged spatially.

By placing links at a specific location on a virtual Loci Desktop, you will be able to find them virtually instantly without navigating menus or resorting to using a search engine.

Create Multiple Desktops

Desktops need not get cluttered, because you can create as many desktops as you wish. Create one for work, on for entertainment, etc.

Share or Don't Share

Desktops can be public so that anyone can see your links, or made completely private so that only you can see them.

Private desktops are so private that if you are not logged in, nobody will even know they exist!
